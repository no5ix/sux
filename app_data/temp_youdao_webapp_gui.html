border [ 'b?:d? ]

  • n.
    • a line that indicates a boundary

      同义词： boundary line borderline delimitation mete

    • the boundary line or the area immediately inside the boundary

      同义词： margin perimeter

    • the boundary of a surface

      同义词： edge

    • a decorative recessed or relieved surface on an edge

      同义词： molding moulding

    • a strip forming the outer edge of something

      "the rug had a wide blue border"

  • v.
    • extend on all sides of simultaneously; encircle

      同义词： surround skirt

    • form the boundary of; be contiguous to

      同义词： bound

    • enclose in or as if in a frame

      同义词： frame frame in

      同义词： edge

    • lie adjacent to another or share a boundary

      同义词： adjoin edge abut march butt butt against butt on

border /?b??d?/ CET4 TEM4 ( bordering, bordered, borders )

  • 1. 

    N-COUNT The border between two countries or regions is the dividing line between them. Sometimes the border also refers to the land close to this line. 边界; 边界地区

    例：

    They fled across the border.

    他们越过边界逃走了。

    例：

    Soldiers had temporarily closed the border between the two countries.

    士兵们已暂时封闭了两国的边界。

  • 2. 

    V-T A country that borders another country, a sea, or a river is next to it. 与…接壤

    例：

    ...the European and Arab countries bordering the Mediterranean.

    …地中海沿岸的欧洲国家和阿拉伯国家。

  • 3. 

    PHRASAL VERB Border on means the same as . 与…接壤

    例：

    Both republics border on the Black Sea.

    这两个共和国都与黑海毗邻。

  • 4. 

    N-COUNT A border is a strip or band around the edge of something. 饰边

    例：

    ...pillowcases trimmed with a hand-crocheted border.

    …镶有一道手工钩制花边的枕套。

  • 5. 

    N-COUNT In a garden, a border is a long strip of ground planted with flowers, along the edge of a path or lawn. (小路或草地边) 狭长花坛

    例：

    ...a lawn flanked by wide herbaceous borders.

    …一片两侧有草本植物花坛的草坪。

  • 6. 

    V-T If something is bordered by another thing, the other thing forms a line along the edge of it. 给…镶边

    例：

    ...the mile of white sand beach bordered by palm trees and tropical flowers.

    …环绕着棕榈树和热带花卉的一英里白色沙滩。
